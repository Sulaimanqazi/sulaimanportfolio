Job Interview Tips

1. When asked about your skills that relate to the job, use the STAR method. Set the scene, explain the task, tell your action, and then share the result
2. When asked about your weaknesses, never choose a weakness central to the job
3. Arrive to the interview 10 minutes early
4. Prepare for the interview by researching common interview questions, and listing five questions to ask the interviewer about the job
5. Write an interview thank-you letter, reminding the interviewer of your qualities after the interview
